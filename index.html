Gary Steven Goseland

December 30, 1951 - August 21, 2025

In God's Care

Gary Steven Goseland, beloved father, grandfather, and friend, passed away peacefully on August 21, 2025, at the age of 73. Steve, as he was known to family and friends, was born on December 30, 1951, and lived a life filled with love, laughter, and unwavering faith.

Steve was a man of deep character and gentle spirit who touched the lives of everyone he met. His warm smile and kind heart made him a friend to all, and his wisdom and guidance were cherished by his family and community.

Throughout his life, Steve demonstrated remarkable strength and resilience, facing challenges with grace and determination. He was a devoted family man who found his greatest joy in spending time with his loved ones, sharing stories, and creating lasting memories.

Steve's faith was the cornerstone of his life, providing him with comfort, purpose, and the foundation for his many acts of kindness and generosity. He lived his life as a testament to his beliefs, always ready to lend a helping hand or offer words of encouragement to those in need.

Though Steve has left this earthly life, his spirit lives on in the hearts of all who knew and loved him. His legacy of love, faith, and compassion will continue to inspire and guide us in the days ahead. May he rest in peace, surrounded by the eternal love of God.
